Frequently Asked Questions

Your Guide to Navigating Treatment in Japan

Stay & Entry

What are the requirements for entry and stay?

Many advanced treatments in Japan can be completed within the **90-day Temporary Visitor** stay. We coordinate your medical schedule and logistics to ensure maximum efficiency within this timeframe.

*We propose tailored plans that aim for the best results without the need for complex long-term visa procedures.

Treatments

What treatments can you coordinate?

We facilitate access to a wide range of advanced medical services. Popular consultations include, but are not limited to:

  • ●Feasibility Review
  • ●Proton Therapy
  • ●Immunotherapy
  • ●Hyperthermia Care
  • ●Health Checkups
  • ●DC Vaccine

Accommodation

How is my stay arranged?

We provide **curated recommendations for your stay** based on your medical schedule and lifestyle needs, focusing on proximity to clinics and comfort for your family.

*Note: We provide information and referrals for accommodations. While we guide you through the process, the final booking contract will be held by the client.
