Stay & Entry

Entry and stay requirements depend on your nationality and treatment plan. We focus on non-medical coordination with medical institutions. For visa or immigration rules, please consult official government sources or a qualified professional.

Accommodation

At this stage, we do not arrange or recommend accommodations. Clients book accommodations independently.
